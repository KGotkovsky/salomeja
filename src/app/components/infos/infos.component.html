is the design project developed by Paris based designer Saloméja Jacquet (b. 1994, Paris). Between research and making, SLMJA involves crafting clothing, textile and seat-like principles, as well as final pieces as a service. SLMJA seeks to experiment with shape and surface using soft materials—making garments and objects in a same way, in relation to the body as a common structure. After taking place in functionality, those textile objects tend to keep the energy of an organic process with its craft-like qualities.

BIOGRAPHY

Saloméja Jacquet was born in 1994 in Paris. She graduated in 2017 from HEAD (Geneva, CH) in fashion design and accessories, after a two year-degree in fashion design in France.
